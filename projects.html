Rotary car parking system using RFID
The main aim of this project is to reduce the parking space required to park the car. Me and my teammates made this project in which we can park 6 cars in the space of 2 cars using rotary car parking system. In this system there are 6 slots for parking 6 vehicle and once one of the slot is occupied the occupied slot rotates and make access for the empty slot in which another vehcile can be parked. In this system we have even used RFID card and scanner. The particular card is attached to each one of the slots and as the driver enter the card number in which he/she has parked the car the slot rotates and the driver can take out his vehicle from the parking.
Four Wheel Steering Mechanism
The main aim of this project is to rotate all the four wheels of the vehicle which is also basically known as zero turning radius. This project helps the driver to park the vehcile or turn the vehicle in between the obstacles. The driver can also move the car in vertical/horizontal/diagonal direction without actually turning the vehicle but just with the help of four wheel steering system.
